Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. KikaQuickTools is built to respect your data and ensure a secure, no-tracking experience.

🔒 No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or files. All tools run entirely in your browser.

🧠 No Accounts or Logins: You can use all tools anonymously — there’s no need to sign up or create an account.

📁 Your Files Stay on Your Device: Whether you're compressing an image or analyzing a text, all processing happens locally in your browser.

📊 No Analytics or Tracking Scripts: We do not use cookies, analytics services, or third-party trackers. We believe in a cleaner, safer web.

🤝 Open by Design: Our mission is to build privacy-first tools you can trust. We’re transparent about how our tools work, and we welcome feedback from the community.

🛡️ Changes to This Policy: If we ever update our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page with a clear timestamp.
